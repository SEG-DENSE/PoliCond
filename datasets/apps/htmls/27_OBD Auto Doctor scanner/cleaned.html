If you don’t understand this policy or have any questions about anything we have described here, please reach out to us for help.

Information do we collect from you

OBD Auto Doctor collects information about you when you visit, use and interact with OBD Auto Doctor.

Registration

When you register to use OBD Auto Doctor, we will ask you for your email address and password of your choosing. Your password will be maintained on our system in a secured form. You may also register through your Apple or Google account. If you choose this method, we receive and collect your email address and your Apple or Google identification number accordingly. We use the collected personal data that we obtain at registration for the sole and exclusive purpose of providing our services to you and allowing you to use our service.

Using the services

When you use our services, we may collect personal data from you. We collect information about the use of your account, such as how often you log in and other information related to your registration and use of our services. We use this information internally to deliver the best possible services to you.

Using the mobile applications, you may need to grant the application access to your location to use Bluetooth Low Energy. In this case, the application will not acquire or use your actual location in any way. However, permission is needed to access Bluetooth Low Energy and communicate with the BLE adapters. Android is designed to work this way. Monitoring the GPS speed the location access is required. In this case, your actual location is not used in any way, but only the speed information is accessed.

When you use our services, we collect certain log information. This information usually includes the browser type or app version you used, your Internet Protocol address, and the date and time of day. As a basic matter, we need this information to help us provide our services to you. In addition, we log this information for internal business purposes. The log information is also vital for security, audit, and quality improvement purposes.

We also use third-party analytics packages for our website.

Communicating with us

We may use your contact information to communicate with you about service activity, provide support, and send you other information such as product updates and announcements. These communications specifically won’t be marketing communications but will instead be informational items. You won’t be able to opt-out of these important messages. You may also be asked questions about our services. We collect this information to help you with anything you need and continue improving our service.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser by websites you visit. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Cookies contain information to help the website operate and provide you with the service.

We do not engage in or support cross-service tracking. The only cookies we use are essential ones. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics or marketing cookies on our domain. Disabling cookies in your browser would lead to improper performance of the website.

Your rights

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

You have the right to know what we know about you and obtain your information.
