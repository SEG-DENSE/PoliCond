Privacy policy

Space Mushrooms, the owner of Sweet Home 3D software and copyright as well as sweethome3d.com web site, doesn't collect private data on sweethome3d.com web site, except for users registered in the forum. Traffic logs stored on the server are not public and sweethome3d.com doesn't show any advertising.
Nevertheless, most pages published on sweethome3d.com web site display Sourceforge logo, a few pages show some videos hosted elsewhere, and the forum lets users share images hosted on other web sites. These images and videos come from sourceforge.net, youtube.com, dailymotion.com and other web servers, possibly letting the owners of these web sites the ability to track users' visits on sweethome3d.com web site at their will.

For registered users, data stored on sweethome3d.com consist of:

  • the information given at registration time (at least a login, an encrypted password and an email)
  • their messages posted in the forum
  • the online homes they created in Sweet Home 3D Online or exported in Exported homes manager.

Homes data is private unless the user explicitly agrees to share it publicly. Each user can retrieve his own data by himself or request its removal if needed, by writing at info@sweethome3d.com. Space Mushrooms guarantees that all necessary measures are taken to ensure that users' homes data managed under eTeks previous web site ownership will never be used for any purpose without informing users.

Apps Distributed Through Third-Party Stores
The information about users who download and install Sweet Home 3D or Sweet Home 3D Mobile from the Mac App Store, App Store, Microsoft Store, Google Play, or Amazon is primarily managed by these companies. Space Mushrooms does not receive personally identifiable data about users from these platforms.

Analytics and Tracking in Apps
Our iOS and macOS versions of Sweet Home 3D include Firebase Analytics for app improvement purposes. Firebase Analytics collects data such as anonymous app usage statistics and crash reports. This data is not linked to your identity and is used solely to enhance app functionality and user experience. No personal data, such as names or emails, is collected by us through Firebase.
