When you use our Services, we and our third-party partners collect information about you and your mobile device(s) through cookies and similar technologies:

Cookies help us and our third-party partners track and understand how you interact with our Services, provide a more personalized experience, allow you to more easily login to the Services, and provide you with advertisements based on your browsing activities and interests.
