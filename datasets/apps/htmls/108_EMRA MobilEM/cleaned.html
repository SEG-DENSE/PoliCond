Privacy Policy

The Emergency Medicine Residents' Association (EMRA) respects the privacy of its members and other visitors to its Web site. EMRA strongly believes that if electronic commerce and online activities are to flourish, consumers must be assured that information provided online is used responsibly and appropriately. Most data EMRA collects are used only to help EMRA better serve its members. It is our general policy to collect and store only personal information that our members and visitors knowingly provide. If our privacy policies change, we will notify all users by e-mail or a special announcement placed on the Web site.
