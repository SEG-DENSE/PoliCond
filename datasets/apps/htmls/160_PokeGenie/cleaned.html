Privacy Policy

We (“Poke Genie Inc.”) are providing this Privacy Policy to explain our practices regarding the collection and use of information within the app Poke Genie.

COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION

Poke Genie does not collect any personally identifiable information.

Poke Genie uses third-party services to track app usage data and crash reports, as well as serve ads. Please review the privacy policy of these third party services for more information regarding how they handle personal data.

Analytics & Crash reporting

  • Google Firebase

Ad networks

  • Google AdMob
  • Amazon
  • AppLovin
  • Verve
  • InMobi
  • AdColony
  • liftoff
  • Mobilefuse

USE OF CAPTURED SCREENSHOTS

Poke Genie only uses the screenshots to calculate IV, never for any other purpose. Poke Genie does not send your screenshots over the Internet, except through bug report emails that you may send.

POKE GENIE IVISION

IVision uses iOS’s “Screen Recording” feature to detect Pokemon information from the screen. Information is only used to assess Pokemon, never for any other purpose.

POKE GENIE KEYBOARD

  • Poke Genie Keyboard does not log user information. Nothing you type will be transmitted.

  • Poke Genie Keyboard requires the “Full Access” permission to access the latest screenshot thereby calculating IV, sync scan data between Poke Genie app and Poke Genie Keyboard, access the system clipboard (for pasting text), and retrieve Pokemon stat updates from server.

DELETION OF POKE GENIE RAID-ASSOCIATED DATA

Poke Genie raid profile can be securely and irreversibly deleted by the following steps:
App menu -> Profile -> Delete Account

REVISIONS TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately upon posting on this page.
