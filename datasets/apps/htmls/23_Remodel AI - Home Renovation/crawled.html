Privacy Policy

Last updated August 23 2022

This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of ReImage AI in connection with the ReImage mobile application (the “App”) and reimage.ai website (the “Site”).

TLDR

Here are the basic points that are important if you don’t want to read the fine print:

  • The App is a photo editor that allows uses cloud-based AI models to edit photos.
  • The App only uploads to the cloud the photographs that you specifically select for editing.
  • Photographs are only cached in the cloud up to 1 hour from the initial upload time.
  • We may use third-party cloud providers (eg Google GCP, Amazon AWS) to process images.
  • Photographs uploaded are used only to to provide image editing functionality in The App and further improvement of the AI models.
  • The App and The Site contain no advertising. We have no advertising partners and share no information with advertisers.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please email privacy@reimage.ai

Personal Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect information about you, including:

  • Photographs you provide when you use the App. We obtain only the specific images you chose to modify using the App. The App only has access to the specific photo(s) selected for editing and will not request access to the entire camera roll nor request access to the camera.
  • App usage information, such as information about how you use the App and interact with us, including your preferred language, the date and time when you first installed the App and the date and time you last used the App.
  • Purchase history, if you choose to purchase an App subscription, such as confirmation that you are a paid subscriber to the App.
  • Device data, such as your computer and mobile device operating system type and version number, manufacturer and model, device ID, push tokens, browser type, screen resolution, IP address (and the associated country in which you are located), the website you visited before visiting our Site; and other information about the device you are using to visit the App.

With your consent. In some cases, we may specifically ask for your consent to collect, use or share your personal information, such as when required by law.
