USE OF COOKIES

What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our App or website. These files are used to enhance your user experience, remember your preferences, and analyze your activity within the App or on our website. Cookies can be either session-based (temporary) or persistent (stored for a longer period).

Types of Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the App or website to function properly. They enable core features such as security, login functionality, and preferences management.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our App to remember certain user preferences, such as language or location, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how users interact with our App or website. The data collected by these cookies is anonymous and used to improve the functionality and user experience of our service.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests. They may track your browsing behavior and allow third-party ad networks to display targeted ads. We may use this data to improve our marketing campaigns and display personalized ads both within the App and across other platforms.

Your Consent to Use Cookies
By using our App, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Privacy Policy. For cookies that are not strictly necessary for the functioning of the App (such as advertising cookies), we will ask for your consent prior to their activation.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences
You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. Most devices and web browsers allow you to set preferences for cookies, including:

  • Blocking cookies entirely.
  • Allowing cookies only from specific sites.
  • Deleting cookies after a session ends.

If you choose to block or delete cookies, some parts of the App or website may not function as intended, and you may experience limited access to certain features.
