Nomi.ai - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2023

Glimpse.ai, Inc. (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the Nomi.ai application (the “Service”). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of personal information from our users: a. Account Information – When you create an account with us, we collect information associated with your account, including your name, contact information, account credentials, payment card information, and transaction history. b. User Content – When you use our Services, we may collect information that you provide to our Services. This is necessary for providing core functionality of our Service. We may use anonymized User Content to train our AI and improve our services. c. Usage Data – We automatically collect information about your use of the Services, such as the types of content that you view or engage with, the features you use, the actions you take, time zone, country, dates and times of access, user agent and version, type of computer or mobile device, computer connection, IP address, and the like.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for authenticating users and basic tracking and analytics. By using the Service, you consent to the use of cookies.

3. Analytics

We may use a variety of online analytics products that use cookies to help us analyze how users use our Services and enhance your experience when you use the Services.

4. User Rights

You have the right to correct any personal information we hold about you. You cannot edit past chat messages. If you would like to request a full account deletion, please contact us at support@glimpse.ai.

5. Age Restrictions

Users must be 18 years or older to create an account.
