If you would like to delete your account to remove all data history, please follow these instructions.

From the PicCollage Website

  • Log into your PicCollage account
  • Go to the Settings Page
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and select “Delete Account”
  • Click “Delete Account”
  • Follow the instructions to delete all of your data on our server

From the PicCollage App (iOS and Android)

  • Tap the “=” menu button in the top left corner
  • Tap the PicCollage Community button
  • Turn on the “Social Network” toggle if it is not already active
  • Log into your PicCollage account
  • Tap the Edit Account Info button
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap “Delete Account”
  • Follow the instructions to delete all of your data on our server
