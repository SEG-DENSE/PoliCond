1        How do We collect your personal information

Personal information" (also known as "personal data") refers to the information related to identified or identifiable natural persons (hereinafter referred to as "Data Subjects"). l  Event Information: When you participate in our research, study, marketing, or other events, you need to provide us with the relevant information about such events, such as the delivery address and contact information provided by you such marketing events.

(2) Information automatically collected by us

When you use Our Services, our platform may automatically collect and generate the personal information in connection with your use of Our Services.

l  Usage Information: We will automatically collect the information about your interaction with Our Services, including records of your browsing, playing, searching, bookmarking, scheduling, sharing, downloading, commenting, and advertisement interactions (such as time, location (inferred from the IP address) and other descriptive attribute information), application usage (such as usage duration and frequency), application crashes and performance data, as well as other interaction information related to your interaction with Our Services.

l  Advertisement Information: We will automatically collect your advertisement identifiers (ADID, GAID, PPID, and IDFA), IP address, and records about you watching advertisement or engaged in other advertisement interaction on our platform, and may also generate your Rampid, UID2, and other privacy protection identifiers using your telephone number or email, so as to display advertisements (including personalized advertisements) to you in Our Services.

l  Device Information: We will automatically collect the information related to your device, including:

o    Device or other identifiers: device ID, ICCID, Android ID, IDFV, and advertisement identifiers (ADID, GAID, PPID, and IDFA);

o    IP address, Mac address, Bluetooth address, and DHCP;

o    Device parameter information (including hardware and software): device model, system version, resolution, network information, network operator, date and time, time zone, country/region, language, network status, signal strength, disk/memory capacity, CPU information, device name, battery status and power, browser information (such as browser model, language, time zone, and screen height and width);

o    List of application software.

l  Location Information: We will automatically collect the approximate location information about you, mainly inferred based on your IP address.

l  Speculation Information: We may speculate on certain information about you, including your age range, gender, channel preferences, and video album preferences.

l  Cookie Information: We use cookies and other similar technologies to manage and operate Our Products and/or Services, and automatically collect the cookie-related information. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for details.

Visitor User: In case you use Our Services but have not become a registered user, We will still collect your usage information, advertisement information, device information, location information, and cookie information.

(3) Information from third parties

l  Personal Profile Information: When you log into Our Services with a third-party platform account (such as Facebook, Google, Apple ID, and Astro ID), We will obtain your personal profile information from such third-party platform account, including username, profile picture, telephone number, and email, subject to your authorization.

(4) Publicly available information

l  Contents on Social Media Platforms: We will obtain the contents of public posts from social media platforms (such as Facebook), such as your sharing, commenting, or liking of social media contents related to IQIYI, or your interactions generated on social media pages related to IQIYI.

l  Information from Other Public Websites or Public Sources: To the extent permitted by applicable data protection laws, We may collect your publicly available personal information from other public websites or public sources.

We have adopted the security technology measures that comply with the applicable data protection laws and industrial standards, as well as the supporting protection measures at multiple levels such as organizational structures and management systems to protect the security of your personal information, in order to minimize the risks of leakage, damage, misuse, unauthorized access, unauthorized disclosure, and alteration of personal information (hereinafter referred to as "Data Security Incidents"). We have set up an IQIYI Security Emergency Response Center, which has developed multiple security management systems and emergency response plans, clarifying the reporting and emergency response procedures for security incidents. You have the rights to your personal information as specified by applicable laws and regulations, which usually include the following types. (3) Right to correction

You have the right to request us to correct, supplement, or update any inaccurate, incomplete, or expired personal information related to you.

You can correct some of your personal information (such as your account information) at any time by logging into your IQIYI account. You can also contact us to request correction, supplementation, or update of your personal information.

(4) Portability right

Under specific circumstances specified by the applicable laws and regulations (such as when the Legality Basis for processing of personal information is your consent or necessity for conclusion or performance of a contract between you and us), you have the right to request us to provide your personal information in a structured, commonly-used and machine-readable format, and to transfer your personal information to other data controllers designated by you.

You can contact us to request the exercise of data portability rights.

(5) Right to erase

Under specific circumstances specified by the applicable laws and regulations, you have the right to request us to erase your personal information, such as when the processing purpose has been achieved, when you withdraw your consent and there is no other Legality Basis, or when the processing activity is illegal. In accordance with the applicable laws and regulations, We will promptly erase or anonymize your personal information. In case your personal information cannot be immediately erased or anonymized due to legal requirements or technical limitations, We will securely store your personal information, ensure that your personal information will not be processed in any other way, and promptly erase or anonymize your personal information after the limitations are lifted.

(6) Right to restrict processing

Under specific circumstances specified by the applicable laws and regulations, you have the right to restrict our processing of some or all of your personal information, such as when there is a dispute over the accuracy of personal information.

You can contact us to request suspension of processing of your personal information.

(7) Right to object

Under specific circumstances specified by the applicable laws and regulations, you have the right to object to our processing of your personal information, such as when the Legality Basis for processing of personal information is legitimate interests or public interests.

(8) Consent and withdrawal of consent

For processing activities based on your consent as a Legality Basis, you have the right to independently decide whether to give consent for our processing of your personal information in accordance with this Policy, and have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

(9) Right not to be bound by automatically-made decisions

You have the right not to be bound by decisions made solely based on automated processing behavior, including user profiling, in case such decisions will have legal or similar significant impacts on you.

We may analyze your preferences and recommend personalized contents and advertisements to you. You can disable personalized recommendations at any time, as detailed in Sections 2.1(5) and 6.1(10) hereof.

You have the right to reject our marketing information at any time.
