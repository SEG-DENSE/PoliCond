I. How Do We Collect Your Personal Information


In accordance with the requirements of laws and regulations, we follow the principles of legality, righteousness, necessity and integrity, and only collect personal information necessary to realize product functions. We generally collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:


1. Personal information required for user registration

You need to register an account when registering and logging in to the APP for the first time. You need to provide your registration information to complete the account registration and create a password, including the country, address, personal email, phone number, working years, area code, postal code, auto repair qualification certificate (domestic), and ASE certificate (overseas) which authenticates the expert qualification. The above information is necessary for you to use the product. If you do not provide such information, you will not be able to use the services of the product normally. If you only need to use basic services such as browsing and searching that can be used in a non-login state, you shall have the right to choose not to register as our user and not provide the above information.


2. Personal information required for the provision of services


2.1. Equipment information

According to your equipment model and granted permissions in the process of installation and use of the APP, we collect and use the information of your equipment, including the equipment serial number, software version, hardware version, equipment serial number, equipment password, lower computer serial number, network status, current equipment time, IP address, Mac address, user update and renewal fees, etc.


2.2. Diagnostic data information

According to your equipment model and granted permissions in the process of installation and use of the APP, we collect and use the information of your equipment, including the data stream, mileage, fault code, freeze frame, vin, model, year, engine, etc.


2.3. Anti-theft data information

2.4 Location Information

According to your equipment model and granted permissions in the process of installation and use of the APP, we will collect your location information to implement some function, for example the VCI/VCMI equipment paring and connection. We may collect ambiguous or precise location related information of your equipment. For example, region, country code, city code, mobile network code, latitude and longitude information and time zone setting. The location information collection will be depended on the equipment you use and whether you have turned on the location feature of your equipment.


3. Personal information required for personalized recommendation

In the process of using the services of your APP, you agree that we will use our own or a third-party technology to integrate, identify and analyze, conduct data portrait or further process your use behaviors (including registration, login, obtaining verification code, clicking, code scanning, binding, etc.) in the process of using the APP, and form label information that can reflect your behaviors, habits, interests or identity characteristics (hereinafter collectively referred to as the “label information”). We will use the label information for personalized push to you, and we will ensure the safe processing of information after aggregation and fusion.


In accordance with the requirements of laws and regulations, we follow the principles of legality, righteousness, necessity and integrity, and only collect personal information necessary to realize product functions.

4. Process personal information that you have disclosed by yourself or others that have been legally disclosed within a reasonable scope;

We will store the personal information collected and generated in China in accordance with the provisions of laws and regulations. If the storage location of your personal information is transferred from China to overseas, we will strictly comply with the provisions of laws and regulations.
